# Code of Conduct

Bank Flow contributors are expected to keep collaboration respectful, technical and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and considerate.
- Assume good intent, but ask for clarification when needed.
- Keep reviews focused on code, behavior, tests and documentation.
- Make space for contributors with different experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues or restrict participation when behavior is harmful to the project or its contributors.
